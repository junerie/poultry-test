About Our Farm

Welcome to Junerie N. Vicoy Farm, where we specialize in raising heritage breed chickens with care and dedication. Our farm is committed to preserving the genetic diversity of traditional chicken breeds while providing high-quality poultry products to our community.

We believe in sustainable farming practices that respect both the animals and the environment. Our chickens are raised in spacious, natural environments where they can exhibit their natural behaviors, resulting in healthier birds and better-tasting meat and eggs.

Our Mission

To preserve heritage chicken breeds through responsible breeding practices while providing our customers with the highest quality poultry products and educational resources about sustainable farming.

Rhode Island Red

The Rhode Island Red is an American breed of domestic chicken. It is the state bird of Rhode Island. It was developed there and in Massachusetts in the late nineteenth century, by cross-breeding birds of Oriental origin such as the Malay with brown Leghorn birds from Italy. It was a dual-purpose breed, raised both for meat and for eggs; modern strains have been bred for their egg-laying abilities. The traditional non-industrial strains of the Rhode Island Red are listed as "watch" by The Livestock Conservancy. It is a separate breed to the Rhode Island White.

Why Heritage Breeds?

Genetic Diversity

Heritage breeds maintain genetic diversity that is critical for the long-term health of poultry populations and food security.

Sustainability

These breeds are naturally hardy and well-adapted to various environments, requiring fewer resources than commercial breeds.

Superior Flavor

Heritage breeds typically offer better flavor and texture in meat and eggs compared to industrial varieties.
